Key Research Article

Title: "Primary familial brain calcification: update on molecular genetics"

Authors: Tadic V, Westenberger A, Klein C.

Published in: Current Neurology and Neuroscience Reports, 2019

This comprehensive review discusses the genetic basis of Fahr's Syndrome (PFBC), including:

  • Identification of major causative genes (SLC20A2, PDGFRB, PDGFB, XPR1)
  • Molecular mechanisms of brain calcification
  • Current diagnostic approaches
  • Potential therapeutic targets

Citation: Tadic, V., Westenberger, A. & Klein, C. Primary Familial Brain Calcification: Update on Molecular Genetics. Curr Neurol Neurosci Rep 19, 100 (2019).
